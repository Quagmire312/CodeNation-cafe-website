Hot drinks


Awaken your senses with the pure intensity of our Espresso. Each shot is a concentrated burst of bold flavor, meticulously extracted to deliver the essence of rich, finely ground coffee beans. Whether enjoyed on its own or as a base for your favorite specialty drink, our Espresso is the perfect pick-me-up for coffee enthusiasts.

Experience pure comfort with our indulgent Hot Chocolate. Immerse yourself in a rich and velvety sea of cocoa, crafted to perfection. With every sip, you'll be enveloped in a warm embrace of creamy goodness that soothes your soul and brings a smile to your face.

Savor the creamy indulgence of our Latte, where rich, smooth espresso meets velvety steamed milk. This harmonious blend creates a delightful balance of robust coffee flavors and a luscious, frothy texture that will awaken your senses and leave you craving for more.

Embrace the ancient traditions of Japan with our Matcha Latte. Vibrant and energizing, this finely ground green tea is whisked into a smooth and creamy blend with steamed milk. The result is a harmonious fusion of earthy flavors and a gentle caffeine boost that will leave you feeling revitalized.

Indulge in the perfect marriage of chocolate and coffee with our decadent Mocha. Rich, velvety chocolate is expertly combined with a shot of espresso, creating a heavenly blend that satisfies both your caffeine cravings and your sweet tooth. Every sip is a moment of pure bliss.

Discover pure serenity with our refreshing Tea, crafted from the finest leaves. Immerse yourself in the delicate aroma and soothing warmth as you take each sip. From the subtle floral notes to the gentle earthy tones, our Tea offers a moment of tranquility in every cup.

Experience the effervescent bubbles dancing on your tongue as you savor the sweet, invigorating flavors. These classic beverages are the perfect companions for any occasion, providing a delightful burst of refreshment.
